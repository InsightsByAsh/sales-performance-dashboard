# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard
### Tools: Excel + Power BI | Dataset: Superstore

## Business Problem
A retail company had 4 years of sales data but no clear way 
to track performance. The goal was to build an interactive 
dashboard that answers key business questions:
- Which region is driving the most revenue?
- What are the top selling products?
- How does revenue trend month over month?

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel — Data cleaning and preparation
- Power BI — Data modeling and dashboard creation

## Key Findings
- Total Revenue: $2.30M over 4 years (2014-2017)
- Total Orders: 5,009
- West Region leads with $0.8M in sales
- Technology is the most profitable category
- Q4 consistently shows highest sales every year

## Dashboard Features
- KPI Cards for Sales, Profit and Orders
- Top 10 Products by Revenue
- Sales breakdown by Region
- Monthly Sales Trend line chart
- Sales by Category pie chart
- Interactive slicers for Year and Region filtering

## Business Recommendations
- Prioritize West and East regions for marketing campaigns
- Increase Technology product inventory before Q4
- Investigate why South region underperforms consistently

## Dataset
- Source: Kaggle Superstore Dataset
- Total Records: 9,995 transactions
- Time Period: 2014 to 2017
